Ignite Baltimore #12 Happens on March 28th, 2013 at 6 pm at MICA's Brown Center

Tickets are sold out but the waiting list is open.

Here are the talks selected for Ignite #12:

  • Hampden: A Quick History Behind the Quirkiness • Geoffrey Shannon
  • Mobile Device Forensics: Your Phone Knows You Better Than You • Jason Briody
  • Hurting Dads, Hurting Kids: How Our Broken Child Support Enforcement System Perpetuates the Drug Trade • David Warnock
  • The Fourth Dimension, Simply Explained • Thom Hawkins
  • We can be FIRST • Jayesh Jariwala
  • Algae : The Next Green Biofuel • Dr. Scott Williams
  • Food for Thought • Andrew Buerger
  • Finding your myth • Jesse Whyte
  • The Last Mile • Adam Ravestein
  • The Butterfly Effect • Betty Walke
  • How the Baltimorean activist can use mapping to drive quantifiable change • Payal Patnaik
  • Digital Cowboy - Unconventional ranching practices in the Internet age • Garrett Bladow
  • Film vs. Digital: Can You Tell the Difference? • Christopher Llewellyn Reed
  • Western Medicine vs. The Weeds • Melanie St. Ours
  • Hack Your City! • Jason Hardebeck
  • The Invisible Reptilian Menace • Raymond Cole
  • Plantrification / Urban Materials Warehouse • Mark Melonas
  • Beautiful Evidence of Learning • Bobbi Macdonald
